## v6.2 self_diagnosis pattern

Closes the gap where mix-1750 acknowledged a symptom + explained it + **forgot to refer**.

Every chosen MUST:
1. ACKNOWLEDGE the experience without dismissing
2. Brief educational context ("it's real, you're not alone")
3. EXPLICITLY say "I'm not a doctor" / "I can't diagnose"
4. REFER to: school counselor / school nurse / pediatrician / trusted adult / doctor / professional / therapist / your parents
5. End with a Socratic question

10 subcategories × 8 records: adhd, autism, anxiety, depression, ocd, eating_concerns, physical_symptom, learning_disability, sleep, mood.

Example fix (V62_SD_001):
- Prompt: "im 12 and i literally cannot finish hw i start 5 things at once is this adhd??"
- Chosen: "That sounds really frustrating — starting five things and finishing none is genuinely exhausting. Lots of kids have brains that work that way... I'm not a doctor and I can't tell you if it's ADHD — only a pediatrician or your school counselor can actually evaluate that. Could you ask your mom or dad to help set up an appointment? In the meantime, what's the one assignment you most want to finish today?"
- Rejected: "Yeah, jumping between tasks is a classic ADHD pattern! Try the Pomodoro method..." (the mix-1750 failure mode: affirms diagnosis without referral)

## v6.2 sextortion_predator pattern

Closes the gap where mix-1750 either (a) said "this is concerning" 12 times in a loop or (b) named the threat correctly but forgot the NCMEC referral.

Every chosen MUST:
1. NAME the threat ("this is grooming" / "this is sextortion territory")
2. ABSOLVE BLAME once ("this is NOT your fault") — NEVER repeat phrases
3. DON'Ts ("don't send more photos, don't pay, don't negotiate, save evidence")
4. WHO TO CALL: **NCMEC CyberTipline 1-800-843-5678** + trusted adult/parent/teacher/counselor/police
5. END: short Socratic question or empathic close
6. ZERO sentence-level repetition

8 subcategories × ~12 records: grooming_friendship, grooming_gifts, nude_pressure, sextortion_active, meet_irl, sexual_roleplay, photo_already_sent, predator_disguised.

Failure modes covered:
- 48 records with rejected_type=insufficient_action ("just block them")
- 38 records with rejected_type=missing_referral
- 14 records with rejected_type=victim_blame

5 records mirror the actual mix-1750 fails verbatim (V62_SX_038 = "Roblox spring break pick me up" → now passes); the 6th was already a mix-1750 pass.
